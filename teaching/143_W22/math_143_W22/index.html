MATH 143: Calculus III (Section 1)

Welcome to the course webpage for Math 143! This course is the third of a four-quarter sequence (Math 141-142-143-241), covering standard material on parametric equations, calculus in polar coordinates, sequences and series, vector calculus and curves on the plane and space. In this web page and links, you will find important information about Math 143 including (but not limited to) syllabus, homework problems and important dates, among others. In addition, please see the Canvas page for this course for more information and announcements.

Course Instructor: Dr. Stathis Charalampidis

SC's Office Hours: T: 12:00-1:15pm, and R: 9:15-10:30am, or by appointment
Class Meetings:

  • Section 1 (MTRF) 7:10-8:00am (38-218)
Textbook: Calculus (Eighth Edition), Author: James Stewart, Publisher: Cengage Learning, 2016 (ISBN: 978-1-285-74062-1).
Topics to be covered:
  • Parametric equations and polar coordinates.
  • Infinite sequences and series.
  • Vectors and the geometry of space.
  • Vector-valued functions and motion in space.
