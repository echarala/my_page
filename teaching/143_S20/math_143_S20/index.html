MATH 143: Calculus III (Section 2)

Welcome to the course webpage for Math 143! This course is the third of a four-quarter sequence (Math 141-142-143-241), covering standard material on sequences and series, calculus in polar coordinates, vector calculus and curves on the plane and space. In this web page and links, you will find important information about Math 143 including (but not limited to) syllabus, homework problems and important dates, among others.

Course Instructor: Dr. Stathis Charalampidis

SC's Office Hours: T and R: 10:30-11:45am and 1:15-2:30pm
Class Meetings:

  • Section 2 (MTRF) 8:10-9:00am
Textbook: Calculus (Eighth Edition), Author: James Stewart, Publisher: Cengage Learning, 2016 (ISBN: 978-1-285-74062-1).
Access to the course/Zoom:

Due to the COVID-19 outbreak, our class will be offered online using using Zoom. Please, use your Cal Poly credentials and the Zoom ID numbers that have already been sent to you in order to attend classes and office hours.

Topics to be covered:
  • Parametric equations and polar coordinates.
  • Infinite sequences and series.
  • Vectors and the geometry of space.
  • Vector-valued functions and motion in space.
